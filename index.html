What is N'STNT?

N'STNT is an innovative app that simplifies the way you dine out. It's as simple as 1, 2, 3!
First, N'STNT conveniently calculates nearby restaurants based on your current location.
Next, N'STNT shows a map of nearby options.
Then, N'STNT effortlessly outputs great restaurant recommendations and sorts them by price, rating & address.
